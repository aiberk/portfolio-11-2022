AR for Printing



During the summer and fall of 2018, I had the incredible opportunity to work with HP's Global Design team in Vancouver, WA to imagine AR capabilities for printers. Throughout my internship, I worked closely with my manager, marketing, user research teams, and PM to create user flows, user stories, and prototypes based on customer needs and insights. During my internship, the lead researcher and I conducted resonance and multiple rounds of usability tests with Usertesting.com to capture insights and opportunities for the future product road-map.

During my 7 month internship at HP, I was able to witness the development of two types of products the HP Tango, and HP Sprocket (pictured below). This experience gave me a lot of insight of the process and hard work of making consumer electronics.

While the details of my work are still under NDA, I'd be thrilled to chat about more deeply about my experience over email or video call.
